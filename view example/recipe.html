Honey Buffalo Chicken Wraps

Recipe

These honey buffalo chicken wraps are healthy and delicious! Loaded with a sweet buffalo sauce, bleu cheese, and crisp, fresh veggies

Ingredients

- 1 pound chicken breast, 1/2 cup buffalo sauce, 1/2 cup honey, 1/2 teaspoon garlic, 1/2 teaspoon onion, 4 burrito-sized tortillas, romaine lettuce chopped, shredded carrots, ranch, bleu cheese'

Preparation

Cut the chicken into one-inch cubes. In a medium-sized bowl, add the buffalo sauce, honey, garlic, and onion. Whisk together and add the chicken to the marinade. Marinate for at least 30 minutes. In a medium-sized skillet add the chicken with marinade. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink at medium-high heat. Layout a tortilla. Add the lettuce and carrots to the center. Add the chicken and top with ranch and bleu cheese. Wrap up tightly and serve
